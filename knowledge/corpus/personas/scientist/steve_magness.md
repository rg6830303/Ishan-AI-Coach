About Steve

Writing: Author of 5 books, including Win The Inside Game and Do Hard Things.  All told, my books have sold over a million copies, been national and international best sellers, and have been translated into more than 25 languages.

 

Performance Coaching: I help performers reach their potential. From athletes to executives to actors and everything in between, I work with individuals to improve their mental performance. I've worked with Olympians and individuals from every major professional sports league.

 

I am also co-creator of The Growth Equation, an online platform dedicated to the understanding and practice of performance and well-being.
